About Leo

Nail artist | Creative dreamer | Your smile maker

Hello, I'm Leo

Welcome to my little corner of creativity. I'm Leo, and I believe that beautiful nails are more than just an aesthetic—they're a form of self-expression, a confidence boost, and a moment of joy in someone's day.

I started this journey with a simple passion: to make people smile. What began as a love for the artistry of nail design has transformed into a mission to create a sanctuary where every guest feels valued, celebrated, and transformed.

At Nails by Leo, you're not just getting a service—you're stepping into a space where your happiness matters. I'm committed to listening to your vision, honoring your unique style, and delivering work that makes you feel like the absolute main character of your own story.
